Teaching Hospital Kalutara is a prominent healthcare institution located in the Kalutara district of Sri Lanka. As the sole tertiary care hospital in the region, it plays a crucial role in providing advanced medical services and education to the local community.
This esteemed institution serves as a vital hub for medical training and research, offering a diverse range of specialized medical services, including surgeries, diagnostics, and treatments for complex medical conditions. It boasts a dedicated team of healthcare professionals, including experienced doctors, nurses, and support staff, who are committed to delivering high-quality patient care.


VISION

“To be a premier District Teaching Hospital that strives to anticipate and respond to the lifelong health needs of the residents of Kalutara and beyond by providing excellent clinical care.”
